# Tutor App — Online Tutoring Platform

Tutor App helps students connect with qualified tutors, schedule sessions, and learn via real‑time video calls. It also supports short‑form educational content to extend learning beyond the classroom.

## Objectives

- Match students and tutors by subject expertise and availability.
- Enable reliable, low‑latency video sessions with robust authentication.
- Let tutors publish short educational videos to reinforce learning.

## Key Features

- Real‑time video calls (WebRTC)
- Authentication + tutor verification
- Educational reels (short‑form content)
- Reviews & basic analytics
