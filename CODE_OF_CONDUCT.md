# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, professional, and collaborative environment.

## Expectations
- Be respectful and constructive in discussions and reviews.
- Focus on technical facts and user value.
- Assume good intent; seek clarity when uncertain.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Introducing security risks intentionally or being reckless with secrets.

## Reporting
- Contact the maintainer privately with concerns. Provide context, links, and steps to reproduce where applicable.

## Enforcement
- The maintainers may moderate issues/PRs and revoke participation for violations.

Thank you for helping build a healthy community.
